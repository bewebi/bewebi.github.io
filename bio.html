Bernie's Bio

Bernie Birnbaum (born June 15, 1995) is a Computer Science Major at Tufts Universty. He was born and raised in New York City, attending Stuyvesant High School. He spent his summers as a camper at URJ Eisner Camp beginning in 2007 and continued to return as a counselor through 2015.

Birnbaum excels academically, posting an A- average at New York City's most reputable public High School, and making the Dean's List multiple times at Tufts University. Outside of class, he is involved with Israel advocacy and sports broadcasting at Tufts. While in high school he played baseball recreationally and ran a dog-walking business which he founded in the 7th grade. Birnbaum is also ranked in the top 3000 pinball players in the world and has flown an airplane twice!
